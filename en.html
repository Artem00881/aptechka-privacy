Aptechka Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 June 2026

Aptechka is an app for managing your home medicine cabinet (medicines, expiry dates, stock, intake reminders). We built it to be privacy-first: your medical data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud. We have no server holding your medical data and no user accounts.

What data we collect

By default, no data is sent to us. Medicines, schedules, intake history, and notes are stored locally on your device (Apple SwiftData). We use no advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Where data is stored

  • Locally on your device — the primary mode.
  • iCloud. Data syncs to your private iCloud database (Apple servers, encrypted) so it is available on your devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Only you can access this data — we cannot see it. It is managed through your Apple ID and iCloud settings.

Features that use the internet (all optional, off by default)

  • Smart photo recognition (AI). If you enable this feature (Premium) and photograph a package when the medicine couldn’t be identified locally, a downscaled photo of the package is sent to our technical proxy and to Anthropic (Claude) to recognize the name. The photo is used only for recognition, is not retained long-term, and is not linked to your identity. Your cabinet and personal data are not sent.
  • Name suggestions (OpenFDA). If enabled, only the text you type is sent — never your cabinet.
  • Medicine leaflets. Loaded from the official Ukrainian registry (drlz.com.ua) by medicine number/name.
  • Server reminders (Live Activity). Disabled in this version. If enabled, your intake schedule (times) and identifiers are sent to our server — medicine names are not sent.

Camera

Camera access is used to scan barcodes and photograph packaging. Image processing happens on the device; a photo is sent over the network only when AI recognition is enabled.

Notifications

Reminders (expiry, refill, intake) are generated locally on the device.

Purchases

The Premium subscription is processed by Apple (StoreKit) and tied to your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your card details.

Third-party services (processors)

  • Apple — iCloud (sync), StoreKit (purchases), notification delivery.
  • Anthropic — only if you enable AI photo recognition.
  • OpenFDA — only if you enable network name suggestions.
  • State Service of Ukraine on Medicines / drlz.com.ua — official registry for medicine leaflets.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete medicines and data inside the app at any time. Deleting the app removes local data. Data in iCloud is removed via your iCloud settings.

Children

The app is a reference and tracking tool for adults and is not intended for use by children; we intentionally do not collect children’s data.
